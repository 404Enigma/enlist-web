Information Gathering & Usage

When you register in Enlist, we ask for information such as your PRN and college email address. Your information is only used internally and won't be shared with others. Enlist collects the college email addresses of those who communicate with us via email, and information submitted through activities such as site registrations. Enlist also collects user data regarding app usage and website. The user data we collect is used to improve Enlist and the quality of our service. We only collect personal data that is required to provide our services.

We process data for analysis using our own scripts and system.
